The C API¶

PW85_DIM¶

The dimension of the physical space (3).

PW85_SYM¶

The dimension of the space of symmetric matrices (6).

void pw85_spheroid(double a, double c, double n[PW85_DIM], double q[PW85_SYM])¶

Return the quadratic form associated to a spheroid.

The spheroid is defined by its equatorial radius a, its polar radius c and the direction of its axis of revolution, n; q is modified in-place with the coefficients of the quadratic form.
