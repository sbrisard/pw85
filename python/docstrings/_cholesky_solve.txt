"Compute the solution to a previously Cholesky decomposed linear system.\n"
"\n"
"Let ``L`` be a lower-triangular matrix, defined by the ``double[6]`` array ``l``\n"
"(see :py:func:`_cholesky_decomp` for ordering of the coefficients). This\n"
"function solves (by substitution) the linear system ``Lᵀ⋅L⋅x = b``, where the\n"
"vectors ``x`` and ``b`` are specified through their ``double[3]`` array of\n"
"coordinates; ``x`` is modified by this function.\n"
"\n"
"This function is exposed for testing purposes only.\n"
